Our Staff

Gael is responsible for all the manual labour along with his partner at LawnCarePro and for driving the car to the location. Gael has been with us since our foundation in 2025 and is a highly valued member of the team. He also keeps his lawn in pristine condition all year round.

Darius has worked with LawnCarePro since 2025. He joined the company after graduating from Heritage College the year before. He has always been passionate about caring for lawns and treats every job like it's being done on his own house. He also maintains a beautiful garden full of flowers and vegetables.

Mike joined the company in 2025 and is currently a site manager. He has worked in all areas of the business. With many years of experience cutting grass—having run his own lawn service as a teenager—his management and planning skills help maintain an excellent team.

Richard is LawnCarePro’s founder and CEO. He created the company in 2025 after running a lawn care business as a kid. His passion for the industry remains strong, and his entrepreneurial spirit continues to drive LawnCarePro’s success.
