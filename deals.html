Details About Our Lawn Care Package

  • Want to bring out the best in your lawn's color, health, and density?
  • Our Essential Lawn Care Package is precisely what you can count on to provide your grass with exactly what it needs to thrive. At the beginning of spring, we begin with a full treatment regimen that involves aeration, overseeding, and fertilizer to encourage growth. Our spring fertilizer is a slow-acting fertilizer that provides your lawn with continuous sustenance during the hot summer season. In autumn, we repeat the treatment to ensure your grass is able to develop a resilient root structure to carry it through the approaching winter.
  • Our fertilizer is top-grade granular and high in nitrogen, phosphate, and potassium – the key to vibrant color and strong growth. A well-rooted lawn is always greener and heals faster. It can even survive challenging weather.

Details About Our Deluxe Lawn Care Package

  • Enhance your lawn experience with our Deluxe Lawn Care Package that offers everything in our Basic Package - aeration, overseeding, and fertilizer application - plus three specialized Fiesta weed control treatments in May, June, and September.
  • At LawnCarePro, we do not prioritize quantity over quality as large firms do when servicing thousands of lawns per year. Rather, we concentrate our efforts on a small shortlist of just a few hundred lawns per year. Moreover, all of our weed control programs are dealt with directly by our two most experienced staff members - one of our firm's partners with more than 20 years of overall lawn care experience, as well as our Regional Manager of Eastern Canada, a seasoned pro as well.
  • Fiesta is a natural, iron-based herbicide that effectively kills broadleaf weeds by blackening their leaves and reducing their strength so that grass grows in its place. Eco-friendly and non-toxic, our product adheres to all environmental regulations, ensuring you a verdant landscape that is completely free of harsh chemicals.
